Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 2 plump garlic cloves
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

1. Cook the spaghetti.
2. Fry the pancetta.
3. Beat the eggs and mix with cheese.
4. Combine everything and serve.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 200g butter
  • 200g sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 200g flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven.
2. Melt the chocolate and butter.
3. Mix the sugar and eggs.
4. Combine everything and bake.
